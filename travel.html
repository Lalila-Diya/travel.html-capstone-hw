Best Places to Visit

Switzerland

Switzerland is a landlocked country in Central Europe, bordered by Germany, France, Italy, Austria, and Liechtenstein, known for its stunning Alpine landscapes and high standard of living. It is a federal republic of 26 cantons, with four official languages—German, French, Italian, and Romansh. While Bern is the de facto capital, Zurich is the largest city, and the country is famous for its neutrality, although it has joined recent international sanctions.

Singapore

Singapore is a sovereign city-state in Southeast Asia, a major global trade hub located at the southern tip of the Malay Peninsula. It is a parliamentary democracy with four official languages—English, Mandarin, Malay, and Tamil—and a diverse, multiethnic population. Since gaining independence in 1965, it has transformed into an advanced economy driven by manufacturing, finance, and its status as one of the world's busiest ports.
